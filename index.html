About Me

I am a master candidate of Computer Science and Technology in BUPT. The M.E. degree is expected to be obtained at the State Key Laboratory of Network and Switching Technology in June 2021. I am currently working with Associate Professor Jiwei Huang and Professor Bo Cheng. Before that, I received the B.E. degree of Computer Science and Technology from BUPT in June 2018. There have been several research papers published or in press, e.g., IJWGS, PPNA, IEEE ICWS, IEEE SCC, and IEEE ISPA.

Research Interests

Service Composition, Performance Evaluation and Optimization, Resource Management, Edge Computing, Internet of Things.
