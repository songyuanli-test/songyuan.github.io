About Me

Hello! I received the B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees in Computer Science and Technology from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), China, in 2018 and 2021, respectively. During my Master's career, I was lucky enough to work with Professor Jiwei Huang and Professor Bo Cheng at the State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switching Technology, Beijing, China. Thus far, I have published articles in international journals and conference proceedings, including IEEE TNSM, IJWGS, PPNA, IEEE ICWS, IEEE SCC, IEEE ISPA, etc. My current research interests include services/cloud/edge computing, performance evaluation, and optimization.

Research Interests

QoS Evaluation and Optimization, Services Computing, Edge Computing, Cloud Computing.
